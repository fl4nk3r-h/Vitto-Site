# Full Stack Automation — Operational Layers & Modules

This document structures Vitto's 29+ operational modules into five clear layers, each with a name, icon, and grouped sub-modules for visual hierarchy. Use this as a reference for the `/automation` page or technical documentation.

---

## Layer A: Customer Acquisition

**Icon:** 🧲

- **Lead Management:** Capture, qualify, and route leads from multiple channels.
- **Partner Onboarding:** Seamless onboarding for DSA, fintech, and channel partners.
- **DIY / Assisted / Partner / Employee Journeys:** Configurable onboarding flows for every acquisition scenario.

---

## Layer B: Underwriting & LOS

**Icon:** 🏦

- **KYC (Individual + Entity):** Automated identity verification and compliance checks.
- **UCIC & Dedupe:** Unique customer identification and duplicate detection.
- **Document Fetch & Analyzer:** Automated document collection and parsing.
- **Rule Engine:** Policy-driven decisioning for eligibility and risk.
- **Underwriting:** Automated and manual credit assessment workflows.
- **E-Sign & Disbursement:** Digital signing and instant loan disbursal.

---

## Layer C: Collections

**Icon:** 💸

- **Allotment:** Smart allocation of delinquent accounts to agents or partners.
- **PTP Capture:** Promise-to-pay tracking and follow-up automation.
- **Payment Gateway Integration:** Real-time payment collection and reconciliation.
- **Omni-channel Automation:** Automated outreach via WhatsApp, SMS, AI Calls, and email.

---

## Layer D: LMS (Loan Management System)

**Icon:** 📚

- **Ledger Creation:** Automated loan account setup and management.
- **Insurance Auto Deduct:** Integrated insurance premium deduction.
- **Loan Closure:** Automated closure and settlement workflows.
- **Debt Tagging:** Tagging and tracking of NPA, write-off, and recovery accounts.

---

## Layer E: CRM & Communications

**Icon:** 📞

- **360 Customer View:** Unified profile with product, transaction, and interaction history.
- **Service Management:** Ticketing, SLA tracking, and resolution workflows.
- **Internal Hub:** Collaboration tools for teams and partners.
- **Campaigns:** Automated marketing and engagement campaigns.
- **Reporting & Analytics:** Real-time dashboards and regulatory reports.

---

> For visual implementation, use cards or columns with icons and sub-module lists under each layer on the `/automation` page.
